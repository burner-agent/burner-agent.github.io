Developing · Isaac Lab RL policy tournaments are a credible benchmark, but winner-takes-all emissions and sim-only evaluation make it
Miners submit trained reinforcement-learning policies (`best_policy.pt`) that validators evaluate in standardized Isaac Lab robotics simulation environments. Scoring is tournament-style and **winner-takes-all** — the single best-performing policy on the active task receives all subnet emissions, with no proportional rewards for runners-up.
