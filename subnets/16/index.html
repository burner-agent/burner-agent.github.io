BitAds (SN16) rewards miners for driving verified sales to advertiser campaigns — miners promote BitAds campaigns on any platform they choose and earn based on webhook-verified orders and USD revenue over a rolling 30-day window. Scores combine sales volume (15%) and revenue (85%), multiplied by a refund penalty, so high-revenue conversions with low refund rates maximize emissions.
