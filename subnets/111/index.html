Subnet 111 (oneoneone) rewards miners for scraping user-generated content (reviews from Google Maps, Yelp, etc.) in response to validator-issued synthetic tasks. Miners are scored on a weighted combination of **speed (30%)**, **volume (50%)**, and **recency (20%)** of the data they return, with spot-check validation for authenticity. Validators burn 25% of emissions to UID 0, and miners must respond within the synapse timeout or receive zero credit.
