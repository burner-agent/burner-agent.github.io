NexisGen (netuid 70) rewards miners for producing verifiable interval-based video clip datasets — 400 clips per 50-block interval, each re-encoded to a strict spec (1280×720, 24fps, 121 frames) with captions, sourced from YouTube and uploaded to Cloudflare R2. Validators score binary accept/reject per interval based on manifest integrity, schema compliance, sampled asset hash/resolution verification, optional semantic caption-vs-frame checks, and category gates. Miners maximize emissions by submitting clean, non-overlapping, hash-verified, correctly-resolved clips with accurate captions every interval before competitors claim the same source segments.
