Miners build predictive simultaneous translation models that generate translations incrementally as speech is revealed step-by-step. Scored on U_best_early = accuracy * (1/step) to reward correct early predictions; 20% of emissions distributed to all qualifiers proportionally, 80% to arena winners. Maximize by correctly predicting translations before full utterances are spoken.
