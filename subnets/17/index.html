Subnet 17 (404—GEN) rewards miners for producing the best AI-generated 3D models (Three.js JavaScript modules) from text/image prompts. Validators run a "winner-stays-in" competition where a vision-language model judges miner outputs in pairwise duels, and winners must pass a verification step where their Docker image is re-run on a 4×H200 pod to confirm the outputs are reproducible. Only the round winner earns the emissions, with a weight decay applied if the same miner keeps defending.
