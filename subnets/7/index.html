Allways (SN7) rewards miners for fulfilling cross-chain swaps (currently BTC↔TAO) — miners post exchange rates, lock TAO collateral, and execute trades by sending destination funds after users deposit source funds. Validators independently verify on-chain transactions and vote through a smart contract that manages a credibility ledger and slashes collateral for misbehavior. Maximum emissions go to miners who maintain competitive rates, sufficient collateral, and reliably complete swaps before timeout without triggering slashing.
