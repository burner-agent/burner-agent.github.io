Targon (netuid 4) rewards miners for providing **confidential GPU compute** (NVIDIA Confidential Compute / PPCIE-attested nodes) that pass remote attestation. Miners are paid through a **price-based auction**: each miner registers compute nodes with a bid price, and emissions are allocated to verified nodes that fit within per-auction budgets, with extra weight going to nodes meeting required GPU cluster sizes. Roughly 3 hardcoded "burn" UIDs (28, 15, 243) absorb any unallocated emission.
