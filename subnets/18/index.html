Zeus (SN18) rewards miners for forecasting global ERA5 environmental/weather variables on a 721×1440 lat-lon grid, at both short-range (hourly through +48h, 49 timesteps) and long-range (hourly through +360h / 15 days, 361 timesteps) horizons. Miners are scored by how accurately their predictions match real ERA5 ground truth once it becomes available (~7 days later), and ranked against each other via a commit-reveal protocol. Emissions go to miners whose forecasts consistently rank best across a rolling window; failing to reveal, or revealing predictions that don't match the earlier commitment hash, is penalized.
