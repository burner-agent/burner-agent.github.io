Miners submit structured ethical-dilemma scenario configs that validators run through Concordia generative-agent simulations; the resulting transcripts are used to fine-tune LLMs evaluated on the MoReBench moral reasoning benchmark (500 scenarios, 23k rubric criteria, 5 dimensions). Scoring is based on proportion of rubric criteria met across all evaluated scenarios. Emissions maximize by crafting diverse, high-quality moral dilemma scenarios that produce training transcripts scoring well across all MoReBench dimensions.
