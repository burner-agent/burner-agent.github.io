Developing · Brier-scored binary forecasting with sandboxed agents is methodologically sound and aligned with Good Judgment Project r
Numinous (SN6) rewards miners for submitting Python forecasting agents that predict binary event outcomes (0.0–1.0 probabilities). Validators execute these agents in sandboxes and score them by **Brier Score** averaged over a rolling window of 100 events, with a **Winner-Takes-All** emission distribution. The single best-calibrated forecaster captures the reward pool.
