Miners train ONNX models that predict US residential real estate sale prices, uploading them to HuggingFace and committing the hash on-chain. Validators score models daily using `1 - MAPE` against fresh sales data the model couldn't have seen, and the winner — the earliest committer within a small threshold of the best score — takes 99% of emissions.
