Task changed: Miners are no longer scored or queried at all — the validator now burns all weight to a single BURN_UID every cycle, suspending the PromptTTS task entirely.
Vocence (netuid 78) rewards miners for deploying PromptTTS models on Chutes that generate speech from prompts describing both content and voice traits (gender, tone, emotion, pitch, speed, accent, etc.). Validators score outputs on content correctness, audio quality, and prompt adherence, then aggregate stake-weighted win rates across active validators. Emissions go winner-take-all to the top miner that beats earlier eligible miners (including an owner base model) by a threshold margin.
