Subnet 8 (Vanta Network, formerly Proprietary Trading Network) rewards miners for submitting profitable LONG/SHORT/FLAT trading signals across Forex, Crypto, and Equities. Miners are scored on risk-adjusted PnL (returns scaled by penalties), with weekly weight-setting based on the prior week's performance. Plagiarism, exceeding 10% max drawdown, or sustained under-performance results in elimination.
