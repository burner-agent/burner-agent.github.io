Swap (SN10) rewards miners for providing liquidity to the TAO↔USDC pool on TaoFi (a Uniswap V3 pool on Bittensor's EVM). Miners are scored by a "concentration score" derived from their LP positions' fee earnings over the past ~24 hours, fetched from the TaoFi subgraph. Top performers earn emissions; miners beyond a rank threshold are zeroed out.
