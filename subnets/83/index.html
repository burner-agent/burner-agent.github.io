CliqueAI (netuid 83) rewards miners for solving Maximum Clique problems on graphs sent by validators — finding the largest fully-connected subset of vertices within a time limit. Miners are scored on a dual metric of **optimality** (how close their clique size is to the true maximum) and **diversity** (algorithmic novelty vs. other miners), with rewards combined via the `CliqueScoreCalculator`. Final weights are an EMA of per-round rewards, so consistent, accurate, and distinctive solutions maximize emissions.
